ABOUT IHREC



With respect to the ongoing health crisis due to the pandemic, this committee is a unique albeit focused platform for health-related policy debate and decision-making. Established by the WHO Director General under the Article 48 of International Health Regulation agreement, Emergency committees evaluates and debates upon public health crises, provides resource and guidance to international healthcare agencies as well as devises policy recommendations to the Director-General of WHO. Although bereft of political intricacies, the committee allows delegates to delve into economic, healthcare and policy research and come up with informed and practical policy recommendations. A rare sight in the Hyderabad Model UN circuit and especially contextual to current global situations, the committee provides delegates the opportunity to explore the intricacies of health emergencies while giving them the power to debate and devise policies that work to ensure public safety.





Agenda

Strengthening trust in government's COVID-19 response measures: special emphasis on political commitments and resourcing of vaccines.
