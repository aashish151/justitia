Letter From
The Secretary Genereal


Greetings!


I, Sarthak Bansal, on behalf of the Secretariat, am honored to cordially invite you to the 2020 edition of the CHIREC Model United Nations, or the CHIREC MUN 2020.


This iteration of the conference aims to provide a platform directed at students of grades 8 to 12 to convey their unique views through debate and discussion on increasingly prominent, constantly changing world issues. We aim to provide a stimulating environment where amateurs and experienced delegates alike can collaborate at a level that promotes growth and learning, research and articulation alone with essential qualities such as diplomacy, bureaucracy and other skills required for success in today’s world, where the aforementioned skills are becoming increasingly important.

The Chirec MUN aims to be a revolutionary instrument in making the Hyderabad MUN Circuit more accessible and open for delegates. We promise to make this online MUN a remarkable and memorable experience this year as well, with the help of our impeccable, hard-working Secretariat and dedicated Organizing Committee.


We hope you attend the conference, ameliorate your skills, and immerse yourself into one of the best MUNs the city of Hyderabad has to offer.



Sarthak Bansal,


SECRETARY GENERAL



About CHIRECMUN


Welcome to the 2020 iteration of the CHIREC Model United Nations, a platform to showcase your oratory and research skills. Hosted by one of the best schools in India, managed by an exceptional Secretariat and a dedicated Organising Committee, and moderated by an adept Executive Board, this conference is one that is sure to be remembered by all those attending. A 3-day long event full of meaningful and thought-provoking debate and discussion, it will help delegates to develop crucial skills required in today’s world. Fascinating agendas and unique committees make this edition one that is not to be missed by both amateur and experienced delegates alike. We hope to provide an enriching experience to everyone taking part in the CHIREC MUN 2020.
