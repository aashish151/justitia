ABOUT UNHRC



UN Human Rights Council Established in 2006 by the General Assembly, The Human Rights Council seeks to protect and promote human rights around the world. The 47 member states discuss and make recommendations about situations involving human rights violations, and hold countries accountable for their actions relating to human rights through country-specific reports and special procedures. Delegates will be challenged with understanding current human rights violations and coming up with solutions that are actually applicable. This involves nuanced understanding of the nature of human rights themselves, and of situational perception and approaches to human rights. The HRC will compel delegates to assume various perspectives in order to make practical recommendations about volatile situations involving human rights violations. .





Agenda
